Hubbard High School Clubs

Welcome to SWIT & MakerspaceClubs

At Hubbard High School, we offer a variety of clubs to engage students in technology and creativity. Our Chi-Craft (Minecraft Education), SWIT (Students Working in Technology) and Makerspace clubs provide opportunities for students to explore their passions, collaborate on projects, and develop new skills.

Available Clubs

Club Name Description Meeting Time
Chi-Craft Club Chi-Craft is a CS club where students practice creative story-telling and design concepts while learning programming skills with MakeCode. There are around 11 students active in the club. Tuesdays at 3:30 PM - 5:00
SWIT Club Students Working in Technology (SWiT) is CS/IT club that allows students to learn about computer hardware, build a computer, 3D print and obtain Industry Recognized Certifications. There are around 19 students active in the club. Thursdays at 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM
Makerspace Club A creative space for students to work on DIY projects, 3D printing, woodworking, and other hands-on activities. There are around 9 students active in the club Wednesday at 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM
Fridays at 2:30 PM -5:00 PM
